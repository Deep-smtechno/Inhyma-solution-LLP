Automatic Pouch Packing Machine

High-speed automatic pouch packing machine designed for packaging snacks, spices, grains, pulses, sugar, salt, and powder products. Features servo-driven technology for precise filling and sealing with minimal product waste.

Key Features

PLC-controlled fully automatic operation
Servo-driven film pulling for accurate bag length
SS 304 food-grade construction
Touch screen HMI for easy parameter setting
Auto-diagnostic & error display system
Multiple bag styles: center seal, 3-side seal, pillow pack

Technical Specifications

SpeedUp to 60 pouches per minute
Bag Width60 – 250 mm
Bag Length80 – 400 mm
Filling Range10g – 1000g
Film WidthMax 500 mm
Power3 kW, 220V/380V, 50Hz
MaterialSS 304 (food-contact parts)
Weight~600 kg
Dimensions1200 × 900 × 1800 mm

Applications

🍿 Snacks 🌶️ Spices 🌾 Grains 🧂 Salt/Sugar 💊 Powder ☕ Tea/Coffee 🥜 Namkeen 🍬 Candy
