Our Story

Building India's Industrial Future

INHYMA Solutions LLP was founded with a clear vision — to become India's most trusted industrial hyper market, making world-class packaging and automation equipment accessible to businesses of every size.

We understand that every business — from a startup to a large enterprise — deserves access to high-quality industrial equipment that can transform their operations. That's why we've built a comprehensive platform offering everything from packaging machines and filling systems to conveyors and warehouse equipment.

Today, we serve 500+ happy clients across 20+ cities, with 5000+ machines installed and a 95% client retention rate. Our success is built on trust, quality, and an unwavering commitment to our customers' growth.
